Once Upon a Time...

In a land far away, there lived a wise old owl who knew all the secrets of the forest. One day, a small curious rabbit approached the owl to ask for wisdom.

The rabbit said, "O wise owl, can you tell me the secret to happiness?" The owl smiled and replied, "Happiness is found in the little things, like a warm day and a friend to share it with."

From that day on, the rabbit began to appreciate the small joys in life, and soon, happiness followed him wherever he went.

The Brave Little Mouse

In a bustling meadow, there lived a little mouse named Max. He was much smaller than the other mice, but he had the biggest heart. One day, a fox threatened the meadow, causing panic among the mice.

Instead of hiding like the others, Max stepped forward and said, "I might be small, but I will not let fear control me!" With courage and cleverness, Max devised a plan that saved the meadow from the fox.

All the mice celebrated Max's bravery, reminding everyone that courage comes in all sizes.
